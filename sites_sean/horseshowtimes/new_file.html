If you've previously entered the current show, the entire entry should populate from a cookie stored on your computer.   To enter a previously entered horse first clear the form, type the horse name in the Horse * box, and click the blue heading HORSE.  This will populate the Owner, Trainer, Payee, Rider1 and Rider2 information boxes excluding classes and charges.  This feature is not case sensitive but only exact spelling matches work.   Owner information can be copied into the Trainer, Payee, and Rider boxes by entering the text self as FirstName and self as LastName and then clicking the appropriate blue header - Trainer, Payee, Rider One and Rider Two.
There's no need to enter your city/state since your Zip/Postal code provides that information.   Clicking the Submit My Entry button or Pay By Credit Card button will save your entry for processing by your show manager.  A red status message should appear immediately under the horseshowtime.com logo above to indicate success or otherwise.  You may do this as many times as you need to and there is no charge for using this form.  Entering an Owner email address will result in receipt of a confirmation PDF email.   If your confirmation PDF email fails to arrive in a timely fashion feel free to resubmit your entry although please allow for reasonable extenuating circumstances. This form itself looks best when printed from Firefox in landscape mode with headers and footers removed.
